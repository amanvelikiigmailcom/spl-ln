## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums)

1. "Bot-free does not mean local" — users discover their cloud tool captures system audio but still sends it to vendor servers. This is the #1 confusion in the market.
2. Meetings containing sensitive data (client details, legal strategy, medical info, hiring decisions) being uploaded to third-party servers without explicit consent.
3. Visible AI bots joining calls make clients and patients uncomfortable, breaking trust.
4. Compliance problems: NDAs, HIPAA, and GDPR restrict what audio data can leave a device or jurisdiction.
5. Hardware anxiety: users worry their older Mac won't handle local AI transcription fast enough.
6. Apps that only work with specific platforms (e.g., Zoom-only) leave users stuck when they switch to Google Meet or in-person meetings.
7. Pricing confusion: many tools charge per minute or per seat, making costs unpredictable for freelancers and small teams.

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
