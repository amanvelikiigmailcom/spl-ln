## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums)

1. Zoom AI Companion only available on paid plans — free and basic users get nothing
2. Bot recorders (Fireflies, Otter bots) feel intrusive and make participants uncomfortable or cautious
3. Zoom's built-in transcription quality degrades significantly with accents, crosstalk, or domain-specific terminology
4. Cloud storage of recordings raises compliance concerns for lawyers, healthcare workers, and HR professionals
5. Meeting summaries from Zoom AI Companion are often vague and miss specific action items or decisions
6. Audio sent to third-party cloud servers for processing — users uncomfortable with confidential calls being stored externally
7. Privacy opt-out on Zoom AI model training is at admin level — individual users often can't control it
