## Competitive strategy for Siplinx article

**Target word count:** 2,800 words (within our target range, beats Competitor 2 by ~65% and focuses on depth over list-length)

**H2 structure we will use** (more comprehensive than all 3):

1. What does "private ai meeting tool" actually mean? (defines the three levels — the gap all 3 miss)
2. Why cloud-based meeting tools carry real legal risk (Otter.ai lawsuit, breach costs, GDPR/HIPAA)
3. Which private ai meeting tools actually process on your device? (comparison table with clear local/cloud breakdown)
4. How on-device ai meeting processing works (technical explainer for non-technical readers)
5. Which type of private meeting tool fits your profession? (decision framework for lawyers, doctors, executives)
6. How to set up a fully private meeting notes workflow with Siplinx AI (numbered steps)
7. FAQ (5-7 questions from PAA + user pain points)

**Our #1 differentiator:** The three-level privacy definition (bot-free vs. local transcription only vs. fully local LLM + STT). All 3 competitors blur these categories. Our article makes the distinction crisp and actionable — which directly addresses the most common user confusion.

**Data competitors don't have:** The Otter.ai August 2025 class action lawsuit example; the fact that BYOK tools still send your transcript to a third-party API. These make the privacy stakes concrete, not theoretical.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Position Siplinx as the only tool that handles both transcription and summarization locally on Mac and Windows, making it the cleaner choice for regulated professionals who can't afford any data leaving the device.
