## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums)

1. Teams' built-in transcription is often inaccurate with technical jargon, product names, and acronyms — users report having to manually correct most of it
2. Notes powered by Loop disappear or become inaccessible after the meeting ends if the wrong settings are used, causing frustrated searches across SharePoint and OneNote
3. Microsoft Copilot for meetings costs $30/user/month on top of existing Microsoft 365 licenses — many teams can't justify the price for AI summaries alone
4. Cloud-stored transcripts raise compliance concerns: lawyers, doctors, and executives worry about confidential meeting content being stored on Microsoft's servers
5. The Teams interface for notes is fragmented across Loop, OneNote, and the Recap tab — users don't know which one to use or where the notes end up

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
