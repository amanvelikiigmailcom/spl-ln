**Target word count:** 2,181 words (matches our WORD_TARGET, beats competitors by ~20-25% in quality even if similar length through better structure)

**H2 structure we will use** (more comprehensive than all 3):

1. Why meetings are still broken in 2026 (sets up with real data — none of the competitors open this way)
2. What does AI actually do in a meeting? (AEO-friendly direct answer competitors skip)
3. How AI cuts meeting time without cutting outcomes (specific tactics)

5. How to run productive meetings with AI in 6 steps (numbered HowTo for schema)
6. Comparing the best AI meeting tools (comparison table — none of top 3 have one)
7. Key takeaways
8. FAQ (5-7 sourced questions)

**Our #1 differentiator:** Every competitor ignores the data privacy angle of AI meeting tools. We own the "your audio goes to the cloud" concern and position Siplinx as the answer for professionals who handle sensitive conversations.

**Data competitors don't have:** The Fortune 2026 stat (executives spend 51% more time in unproductive meetings than in 2019), the UC Today/TechTelligence study (64% rate AI impact at 4/5), and the Atlassian 78% finding on meeting overload. None of the three competitors cite 2025-2026 data.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Position naturally as "the meeting productivity tool for professionals who can't share their audio with a cloud service" — lawyers, doctors, executives, consultants. One section specifically on privacy risk + Siplinx as solution.
