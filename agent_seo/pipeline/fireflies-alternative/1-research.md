## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums)

1. "Privacy emerged as a hot topic in discussions, with some users expressing hesitation over storing sensitive conversation details in cloud-based services like Fireflies AI." (Oreate AI analysis of Reddit sentiment, 2025)
2. Bot visibility — Fireflies sends a visible bot ("Fred") into meetings, which makes clients and prospects uncomfortable, signals surveillance, and disrupts trust in sales and sensitive negotiations.
3. Unpredictable costs — Fireflies charges for AI credits separately from plan fees. Heavy users report spending $95-293/month beyond their base plan on credit overages.
4. Speaker labeling problems — Multiple users tested Fireflies across 30+ meetings and found speaker identification degrades significantly when multiple people talk simultaneously.
5. Cloud lock-in — Users on free and Pro plans have no way to store audio locally; Private Storage is only available at Enterprise pricing, effectively forcing cloud processing even for sensitive content.

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
