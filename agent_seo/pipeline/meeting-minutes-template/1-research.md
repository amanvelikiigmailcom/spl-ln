## Target audience

Project managers, executive assistants, team leads, and administrative professionals who need a structured, reusable system for capturing decisions and action items from meetings.

## Search intent

Informational / Navigational — users want a downloadable or copyable template plus practical guidance on how to fill it in. Secondary intent: discover an AI tool that automates the process entirely.

## Real user pain points (from research and forums)

1. "I write pages of notes during the meeting but nobody reads the minutes afterward — they just ask me the same questions the next day."
2. "We have no consistent format. Every person who takes notes does it differently, so the archive is useless."
3. "I spend 30-45 minutes after every meeting writing up the minutes. It's basically a second meeting."
4. "Action items get buried in paragraphs. People miss their tasks because they don't read to the end."
5. "Board meeting minutes need to be legally precise — informal notes aren't good enough."
6. "I can't write AND participate in the meeting at the same time. I always miss something."
7. "Sharing minutes by email means replies and edits get fragmented across threads."

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.

**Our angle:** Give readers an actual ready-to-use template structure PLUS explain why AI-generated minutes beat manual note-taking (with real stats), PLUS position Siplinx AI as the privacy-safe option for people who can't upload confidential meeting audio to cloud tools.

## Recommended article structure

Based on research, these H2 sections will perform best:

1. What are meeting minutes and why do they matter? (definition + stat hook)
2. What should a meeting minutes template include? (the actual template breakdown)
3. What types of meeting minutes templates do you need? (board, team standup, 1:1, project kickoff)
4. How do you write meeting minutes that people actually read? (format + behavioral tips)
5. Should you use AI to write meeting minutes instead? (manual vs AI comparison table)
6. How to take meeting minutes without missing the meeting (the real pain point)
7. What's the best meeting minutes app in 2026? (tool comparison including Siplinx)
8. FAQ (always last)
