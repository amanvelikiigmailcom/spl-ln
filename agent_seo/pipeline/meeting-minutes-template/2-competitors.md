**Target word count:** 2,352 words (our random target — this beats Fellow's 2,100 and is competitive with WildApricot's 2,700)

1. What are meeting minutes and why do they matter so much? (definition + real stats)

3. What types of meeting minutes templates do you need? (board, team, 1:1, kickoff)
4. How do you write meeting minutes that people actually read? (format tips)
5. Should you use AI to write meeting minutes? (manual vs AI comparison table)
6. How do you take meeting minutes without missing the meeting? (the real pain point)
7. What is the best meeting minutes app in 2026? (tool comparison including Siplinx)
8. FAQ

**Our #1 differentiator:** No competitor covers the privacy/confidentiality angle for meeting minutes. Lawyers, doctors, and consultants can't use cloud-based AI tools to generate their meeting minutes. Our article will address this gap and position Siplinx AI as the only option that works entirely offline.

**Data competitors don't have:** Atlassian's finding that 54% of employees leave meetings unclear about next steps (Flowtrace 2024-2025), and Bloomberg's $375 billion annual cost of unproductive meetings. None of the three competitors cite these stats.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Positioned in Section 5 (AI vs manual) and Section 7 (tool comparison). The privacy angle is the bridge: "cloud AI tools generate minutes from your audio, but that audio gets uploaded to their servers. For confidential meetings, that's not acceptable." Siplinx AI is the answer.
