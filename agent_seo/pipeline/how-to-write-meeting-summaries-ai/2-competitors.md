## Competitive strategy for Siplinx article

**Target word count:** 3,325 words (matches our random target, beats shortest competitor by ~50%, close to Fellow's length)

**H2 structure we will use** (more comprehensive than all 3):

1. What is a meeting summary (and why most teams get it wrong)?
2. What a good meeting summary actually includes
3. How to write a meeting summary step by step
4. How to use AI to write meeting summaries (without sounding like a robot)
5. Meeting summary template you can copy right now
6. Common mistakes that kill the value of meeting summaries
7. How to handle sensitive meetings where cloud AI is not an option
8. FAQ

**Our #1 differentiator:** All 3 competitors completely ignore the privacy dimension. Zero mention of what to do when you can't send your meeting audio to a cloud service (lawyers, doctors, executives, regulated industries). This is a massive gap we own.

**Data competitors don't have:** Real statistics from Fellow's State of Meetings report (unproductive meetings cost $375B/yr, 70% of decisions forgotten in 24 hours, only 37% of meetings use agendas). None of the competitors cite these.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Naturally introduce Siplinx AI in the "sensitive meetings" section as the solution for professionals who need AI-generated summaries without uploading audio to the cloud. Also mention briefly in comparison table as the offline/private option.
