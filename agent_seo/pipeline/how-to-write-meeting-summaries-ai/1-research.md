## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums)

1. Meetings end and nobody writes anything down — decisions evaporate within hours.
2. Meeting summaries are too long and nobody reads them; people want something they can scan in 30 seconds.
3. AI-generated summaries often sound robotic or generic and don't capture actual decisions.
4. Action items are vague ("someone should follow up") with no owner or deadline.
5. Summaries miss the context behind decisions — just logging what was decided without why.
6. Sending the summary too late (days after the meeting) makes it nearly useless.
7. Confidential or sensitive discussions shouldn't go through a cloud service — privacy concern with AI notetakers.

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
