## Competitive strategy for Siplinx article

**Target word count:** 3,200-3,600 words (beats longest competitor by ~20-25%)

**H2 structure we will use** (more comprehensive than all 3):

1. What are sales call notes and why do they matter for closing deals?
2. What should you capture in every sales call note?
3. How do you take notes during a call without losing focus on the conversation?
4. What is the best format for sales call notes in a CRM?
5. How does AI change the way sales teams take call notes?
6. Can you use an AI sales call recorder without sending data to the cloud?
7. What is a good sales call notes template you can use today?
8. FAQ (5-7 questions)

**Our #1 differentiator:** All three competitors completely ignore the privacy and compliance angle. Sales reps at law firms, financial advisors, and healthcare companies can't record calls and upload them to Fireflies or Otter without legal risk. Zero competitors address this. Siplinx AI owns this angle.

**Data competitors don't have:** Salesforce 2025 State of Sales finding that reps spend only 28% of their week selling, and Forrester's stat that 17% of a rep's week goes to CRM data entry alone. None of the three competitors cite real statistics.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Position Siplinx as the solution specifically for sales teams handling confidential conversations. Unlike Gong or Fireflies, Siplinx processes call audio on-device with no cloud upload, making it viable for GDPR and HIPAA-adjacent use cases in sales.
