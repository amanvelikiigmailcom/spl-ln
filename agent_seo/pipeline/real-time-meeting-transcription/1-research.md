## Target audience

## Search intent

Informational / Commercial Investigation — Users want to understand how real-time meeting transcription works, compare tools, and find the best option for their use case (often with a preference for privacy or offline capability).

## Real user pain points (from Reddit/Quora/forums + privacy news coverage)

1. "AI bots joining meetings without asking all participants — not just the host — feel creepy and invasive." (Privacy concern re: Otter.ai lawsuit)
2. "I don't want my confidential client calls uploaded to some startup's servers I've never heard of."
3. "The transcription accuracy drops noticeably with accents or multiple speakers talking at once."
4. "I forget to start the transcription tool or it doesn't auto-join — I miss the first 5 minutes."
5. "Cloud transcription tools cost too much per month for how often I actually use them."
6. "My company's IT policy doesn't allow third-party bots in meetings — but I still need transcripts."
7. "Speaker identification is unreliable when two people have similar voices."

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
