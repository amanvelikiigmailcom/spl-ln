## Real user pain points (from research sources and industry reports)

1. AI summaries miss sarcasm, tone, and nonverbal cues, producing misleading written records of what was actually decided.
2. Cloud-based tools join meetings without explicit consent from all attendees, especially when calendar integrations auto-invite them.
3. Recordings stored on third-party servers may be used to train vendor AI models, putting sensitive business data at risk.
4. Non-native speakers and people with strong accents frequently see badly garbled transcripts that make summaries useless.
5. Most tools push summaries through email to multiple colleagues, spreading confidential discussion far beyond the intended audience.

These should become H2 questions and FAQ entries.
