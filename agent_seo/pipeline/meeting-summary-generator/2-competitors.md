**Target word count:** 2856 words (matches pipeline target, beats competitor 3 and lands between 1 and 2)

**H2 structure we will use** (more comprehensive than all 3):

1. What is a meeting summary generator? (AEO answer block)
2. Why meeting notes fail without automation (backed by $375B stat)
3. What makes a good AI meeting summary tool (selection criteria, not just feature list)
4. Are AI meeting summary generators safe? (cloud risks, 2025 lawsuits — gap ALL 3 miss)
5. How to generate a meeting summary automatically, step by step (HowTo schema — gap ALL 3 miss)

**Our #1 differentiator:** All three competitors completely ignore the legal and privacy risks of cloud-based meeting summary tools. The 2025 Otter.ai lawsuit and Chapman University Read AI ban are known events that directly affect enterprise buyers. We cite both and explain what local/offline processing solves.

**Data competitors don't have:** The $375 billion/year unproductive meetings figure from Fellow's own statistics blog (separate from their tools comparison article). The 71% of senior executives find meetings unproductive stat. The 2025 Otter.ai lawsuit citation from NPR.

**Siplinx AI angle:** Position Siplinx as the only desktop-native meeting summary generator that produces summaries without ever sending audio or transcripts to a cloud server. Hit hard in the "Are AI meeting tools safe?" section and in the comparison table.
